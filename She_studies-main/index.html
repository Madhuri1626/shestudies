About Us

Established in 2023, She studies is a non-profit that focusses on mobilising communities for girls’ education in India’s rural and educationally backward areas. Strongly aligned with the ‘Right to Education Act’ or the ‘Samagra Shiksha’, Educate Girls is committed to the Government’s vision to improve access to primary education for children, especially young girls.

Goal

Our goal is to improve access and quality of education for over 15 million children cumulatively by 2050.
